**I-Bluetooth Volume Manager** ikhumbula amazinga amavolumu kwisigaba ngasinye sezidivayisi zakho ze-Bluetooth futhi iphendule uma zixhuma noma zinqamula.

**Izici:**
* Setha amavolumu omuziki, ocingo, ukhalo lwefoni, nezaziso ngokwedivayisi  
* Thumela ngokuzenzakalelayo imiyalo yemidiya njengo-Play noma okulandelayo  
* Vula izinhlelo uma uxhuma  
* Gcina isikrini siphapheme  
* Vala amazinga amavolumu  
* Buyisela ivolumu ngemuva kokuqumuka  

Azikho izikhangiso, akunagcwanekezo. Imithi eveliwe.  
Ezinye izinto ezengeziwe zidinga ukuthenga okukhokhelwa ukusekela ukuthuthukiswa.

**Qaphela:** Lolu hlelo alukwazi ukwandisa ivolumu noma ukubhukuza imingcele yamavolumu yesistem.

**Izimvume:**
* **I-Intanethi** – yamibiko yezibhagi engakhethekile  
* **I-Bluetooth** – ukuthola uxhumano lwezidivayisi  
* **Izilungiselelo ze-audio** – ukushintsha ivolumu  
* **Ukuqala** – ukubuyisela izilungiselelo ngemuva kokuqalwa kabusha  
* **Wake lock** – ukugcina isikrini siphapheme noma ukulungisa amabanga e-Samsung  
* **Ifasitela lesaziso lesistem** – ukuqalisa ngokuzenzakalelayo izinhlelo (i-Android 10 kuphela)
