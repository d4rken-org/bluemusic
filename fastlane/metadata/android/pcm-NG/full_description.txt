**Bluetooth Volume Manager** dey remember the volume level for all your Bluetooth devices and e go react when dem connect or disconnect.

**Wetin e fit do:**
* Set music, call, ringtone, and notification volume for each device  
* Automatically send media commands like Play or Next  
* Launch apps when device connect  
* Keep screen awake  
* Lock volume levels  
* Restore volume after disconnect  

No ads, no tracking. Na open-source app.  
Some extra features need you pay small money to support the development.

**Note:** This app no fit boost volume pass the system volume limit.

**Permissions wey e need:**
* **Internet** – for optional bug reports  
* **Bluetooth** – to detect when device connect  
* **Audio settings** – to change volume  
* **Boot** – to restore settings after phone restart  
* **Wake lock** – to keep screen awake or fix Samsung bugs  
* **System alert window** – to auto-launch apps (Android 10 only)
