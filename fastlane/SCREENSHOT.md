Prompt for automatic screenshots via /debugbadger:screenshots 

First open the app and ensure we start out on "light mode".
If we are not using the current theme, you can change it via Settings>General>Theme mode

Create a total of 8 phone screenshots in the following order:

* Dashboard in "Light Theme".
* Dashboard in "Dark Theme", change the theme back to "Light Theme" for the next screenshots.
* Device Config Page 1, Click "Configure" on the first (connected) device and take a screenshot of the list header (top of the list).
* Device Config Page 2, Scroll the device config page further down until the reaction card is fully visible and take a screenshot (the card can be found in the middle of the list).
* Device Config Page 3, Scroll down till the end  so that the "Timing" card is fully visible and take the screenshot (card can be found at the bottom of the list).
* App launcher, On the device config page still, center the "Launch app" option in the middle of the screen, click it, and take a screenshot of the app selection screen.
* Autoplay, On the device confing page still, center the "Autoplay Key Types" option in the middle of the screen, click it and take a screenshot of the configuration dialog that pops up.
* Settings, Take a screenshot of the main apps settings index screen
